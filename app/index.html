About Me

Hello world! My name is Bree. I'm a front end developer with an internet marketing background. I am passionate about creating functional and stunning websites that capture your audience's interest.



Internet Marketing, SEO, & Web Development

My fascination with the internet and web development started over 3 years ago when I pursued a Masters in Integrated Marketing Communications. One of my favorite technical courses was about Search Engine Optimization and web analytics, and I became increasingly interested in using website analytics and data to improve websites. My experience further developed when I became the Director of Operations for Send It Rising Internet Marketing. We developed websites for clients, performed SEO, and managed PPC ads.

As I became more knowledgeable about websites and the internet, I decided that web development was my passion. I love that it requires both a technical and creative skill set, and I enjoy bringing ideas to life.

I have completed many courses from Treehouse and Udemy, including full courses dedicated to front end web development and web design. My goals are to expand into back end web development and to develop into a JavaScript expert.

Areas of Expertise

Responsive Web Design

From both a marketing and user experience standpoint, a website needs to look good and function well on different devices. You don't want to lose your customers because the website doesn't work well on mobile.

User Experience & Internet Marketing

A clean website, proper website structure, clear calls to action, and fast load times are fundamental to a good user experience. I incorporate this philosophy into my projects.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) & Analytics

You want your website to be found. Optimizing your website for search engines is key to your audiences finding your site. I practice white hat SEO and have gotten local businesses on the 1st page of Google.

Workflow & Time Management

I use a variety of tools to streamline my workflow: GitHub/git, Node.js, Gulp, PostCSS, and SASS. I'm am constantly trying to learn new ways to boost my efficiency without sacrificing quality.
